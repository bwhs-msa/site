Events


Muslim Interscholastic Tournament (MIST)

The Muslim Interscholastic Tournament (MIST)™ is a fun, educational, interactive program of competitions and workshops geared towards bringing high school students together from around the nation to develop leadership, communication, and other creative skills — all while gaining a deeper understanding of Islam and Muslims.

MIST is a national nonprofit that consists of annual regional tournaments in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Columbus, Dallas, Detroit, Florida, Houston, Nashville, New Jersey, New York City, Philadelphia, Richmond, Seattle, Southern California, Toronto, and Washington, DC.

The winners from each regional tournament qualify to complete at the annual National Tournament. The tournaments lasts two to three days, complete with 40+ competitions, engaging workshops, and judges to evaluate student submissions.

Visit www.getmistified.com to learn more.


Annual Fundraising Dinner

Each year the BWHS MSA hosts a fundraising dinner for a charity. We work together during the years to plan the dinner and is usually hosted in the spring. In the past, we have raised money for Kindness and Care Beyond Borders and the Make A Wish Foundation.
